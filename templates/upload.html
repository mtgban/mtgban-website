Reference

Supported Formats

CSV, Excel (.xls/.xlsx), Google Sheets (public), TCG Collection URLs, Moxfield URLs, or pasted decklists.

Popular exports like TCG, Deckbox, BinderPOS, and Cardsphere are auto-detected. Plain card name lists also work — the most recent printing will be used.

Excel sheets should contain mtgban in the sheet name. Google Sheets must be publicly accessible. The saved URL will persist as a preference.

Data Requirements

Data in any format with a comma or tab separated header. Needs sensible column names like card name and edition at minimum.

Card recognition follows Scryfall notation. If a card has multiple printings (same name, different art), include the collector number or variant.

Max 5MB, up to 350 entries (1000 for Optimizer). Rows with qty 0 are skipped. Identical entries with same condition are merged.

Reading Results

Each card shows prices from all selected stores. A per-store total row appears at the bottom with quantity-adjusted sums.

Below totals: count of cards not found per store, plus an estimated missing value via TCG Low.

The Index Overview shows popular aggregate prices. TCG Direct is condition-adjusted, falling back to TCG Direct Low.

If price or quantity data exists in your upload, it displays above results. Condition data adjusts reported prices and can determine foiling status.

Optimizer

Groups results by store and edition with a theoretical maximum split per store. Similar offers within the margin threshold are all listed.

Each store section shows its offer next to loaded/TCG Low price. The percentage indicates offer strength. Use Load at buttons to push directly to partner storefronts.

The qty multiplier applies only to optimizer operations — loaded data stays untouched. You can download per-store CSVs or remove cards from TCGplayer inventory.

Result Legend

Tinted background — alias was guessed, verify printing via dropdown
Buylist price exceeds your loaded price
🌟 Highest buylist price in 3 months
⚠️ Offer above custom spread threshold

Privacy & Access

No data is stored on the website. Your exports are private to you only.

Some features may be available at certain tiers only. At Modern you can customize the list of Retail stores. At Legacy, you get access to Buylist prices, CSV downloads, and exports. At Vintage you have no download limits and can import custom searches.

Need Help?

If anything doesn't work, post your file in the #feedback channel on the BAN Discord with a URL or screenshot.
