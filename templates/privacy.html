Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

MTGBAN (“we,” “us”) operates price-comparison tools for trading card games. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and which third parties are involved — including our use of affiliate links.

Information We Collect

  • Account information. When you sign in through Patreon or link Discord, we receive basic profile details (such as your name, email, and membership tier) to grant access to member features. We never see or store your Patreon or Discord password.
  • Usage data. Standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages requested) used to operate and secure the service.
  • Preferences. Settings such as theme, mobile view, and hidden stores are saved in your browser via cookies or local storage.

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar browser storage to keep you signed in and remember your preferences. Third parties we link to — including Amazon — may set their own cookies when you visit their sites through our links. You can control or clear cookies in your browser settings, though some member features may not work without them.

Affiliate Disclosure

MTGBAN participates in several affiliate programs operated by our partners. As an Amazon Associate, we earn from qualifying purchases. When you follow a link such as “Available on AMAZON” and make a purchase, we may receive a commission at no additional cost to you. These commissions help support the site; they never change the prices or availability we display.

Third-Party Services

We rely on services including Amazon, Patreon, Discord, and trading-card marketplaces to provide pricing, authentication, and community features. Your interactions with those services are governed by their own privacy policies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only as needed to operate the service (for example, with the authentication providers above) or where required by law.

Your Choices

You can clear cookies, sign out, or stop using member features at any time. To request removal of account data tied to your Patreon or Discord login, contact us through our Discord or email below.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at administrator@mtgban.com.
